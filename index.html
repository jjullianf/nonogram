How to Play

Fill in the grid so every row and column matches its number clues. Each number tells you how many consecutive filled cells appear in that line, in order — with at least one empty cell between each group.

Click to fill a cell · Click again to mark it empty (×) · Right-click to mark directly · Drag to paint multiple cells. Use Note mode to pencil in uncertain cells.
